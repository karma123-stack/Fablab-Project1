Our services

Our FabLab student management will guide you to build your ideas to reality

Custom Prototyping

Bring your ideas to life with our cutting-edge prototyping services. Whether it's a simple model or a complex design, we provide tools and expertise to turn your concepts into reality.

Workshop

Unlock your potential with our hands-on workshops. Learn the latest in fabrication technology and gain skills to build anything you imagine.

Community Services

Our student team helps the community by providing services. We build assistive technologies for students with disabilities to help them live independently.

Orientation

We orient students of our college to join FabLab activities. We teach Python basics and give brief introductions to FabLab and its machines.

Operating Machines

Our student team teaches how to operate machines. We guide students to turn their ideas into prototypes and become more productive.

Products

Our team produces commercial products for lab finances. We take custom orders, build them, and deliver to customers professionally.
