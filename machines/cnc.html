Computerized Numerical Control (CNC)

CNC machines are automated manufacturing tools controlled by a computer program. They can cut, shape, or engrave materials such as metal, wood, and plastic with high precision, making them ideal for industrial applications and prototyping.

Our CNC machine is available for students and makers who want to create highly detailed designs or parts for their projects. Learn how to operate CNC tools and explore the endless possibilities in machining and manufacturing.
