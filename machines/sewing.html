Sewing Machine

An electric sewing machine is a device that uses an electric motor to drive the needle and fabric feed mechanism. It allows for faster, more precise stitching than manual machines and can be used for a variety of fabric-related projects.

At CST FabLab, we offer both basic and advanced sewing machine usage for students and individuals looking to design or repair fabric items. Learn how to sew, make alterations, or create your own unique designs with our high-quality machines.
