Carpentry

Carpentry is the skilled trade of working with wood to create structures, furniture, and other wooden objects. Learn the art of woodwork with our tools and resources at CST FabLab.

From simple projects to more advanced wooden structures, our expert instructors will guide you in learning how to work with wood. You will also get familiar with different types of tools used for cutting, shaping, and finishing wood.
